Ceramic Material Properties

Choose the optimal ceramic material for your specific requirements

Alumina (Al₂O₃)

  • Thermal Conductivity: 24-28 W/mK
  • Dielectric Constant: 9.8 @ 1MHz
  • Breakdown Voltage: 15-20 kV/mm
  • CTE: 7.2 ppm/°C (25-300°C)
  • Applications: Cost-effective solutions, general-purpose substrates

Aluminum Nitride (AlN)

  • Thermal Conductivity: 170-200 W/mK
  • Dielectric Constant: 8.9 @ 1MHz
  • Breakdown Voltage: 15-20 kV/mm
  • CTE: 4.5 ppm/°C (25-300°C)
  • Applications: High-power, high-frequency, laser diodes

Silicon Nitride (Si₃N₄)

  • Thermal Conductivity: 60-90 W/mK
  • Dielectric Constant: 8.0 @ 1MHz
  • Breakdown Voltage: 15-25 kV/mm
  • CTE: 3.2 ppm/°C (25-300°C)
  • Applications: High mechanical strength, automotive

Zirconia (ZrO₂)

  • Thermal Conductivity: 2-3 W/mK
  • Dielectric Constant: 25-30 @ 1MHz
  • Breakdown Voltage: 8-12 kV/mm
  • CTE: 10.5 ppm/°C (25-300°C)
  • Applications: Thermal insulation, sensors
