Bare Ceramic Substrates - Alumina, AlN, Si3N4, SiC, Zirconia

Our bare ceramic substrates provide an unparalleled foundation for demanding applications, offering exceptional high-temperature resistance, superior electrical insulation, and excellent thermal conductivity to ensure peak performance and reliability. While their inherent brittleness and cost present design considerations, these premium substrates—crafted from advanced materials like Alumina (Al2O3), Aluminum Nitride (AlN), Silicon Nitride (Si3N4), Silicon Carbide (SiC), and Zirconia (ZrO2)—are the ideal solution for high-power modules, hybrid circuits, automotive systems, and semiconductor equipment where standard materials fall short.

Product Description

Alumina ceramics (Aluminum Oxide or Al2O3) components are indispensable in the semiconductor and electronics industries, leveraging their high thermal conductivity, superior electrical insulation, and exceptional chemical resistance to ensure reliability in demanding manufacturing processes and advanced packaging applications. From critical semiconductor equipment to protective alumina caps for sensitive electronics, our components provide the essential thermal stability and durability required for peak performance and long-term reliability.

Key Features & Benefits

High mechanical strength High hardness and durability Good electrical insulation Low dielectric constant and dielectric loss High thermal conductivity Excellent corrosion resistance Non-toxic Good surface flatness Zero porosity Low weight
