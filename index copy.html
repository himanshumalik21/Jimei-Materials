Production Floor

Laser Drilling

High-precision laser drilling for creating microvias and intricate patterns on ceramic substrates.

Sputtering

Advanced sputtering technology for depositing thin metal films on ceramic substrates, ensuring strong adhesion and conductivity.

Circuits

Precision circuit patterning using photolithography and etching techniques to create reliable electrical pathways on ceramic substrates.

Plating

High-quality electroplating services to enhance the surface properties of metallized ceramic substrates, improving durability and performance.

Leveling and Polishing

Precision leveling and polishing to achieve ultra-smooth surfaces on ceramic substrates, essential for high-performance applications.

Film Removal Etching

Advanced etching techniques for precise removal of unwanted metal films, ensuring clean and accurate circuit patterns on ceramic substrates.

E-Test

Comprehensive electrical testing to ensure the integrity and performance of metallized ceramic substrates, including continuity and insulation tests.

Soldermask and Silkscreen

Application of soldermask for protection and silkscreen for component identification, enhancing the functionality and aesthetics of ceramic substrates.

Surface Treatment

Specialized surface treatments to enhance adhesion, corrosion resistance, and overall performance of ceramic substrates in various applications.

Laser Shape

Precision laser cutting and shaping to create complex geometries and custom designs on ceramic substrates, meeting specific application requirements.

FQC and FQA

Rigorous Final Quality Control (FQC) and Final Quality Assurance (FQA) processes to ensure each ceramic substrate meets stringent quality standards before shipment.

Packing and Shipping

Secure packing and reliable shipping solutions to ensure ceramic substrates arrive safely and on time to customers worldwide.
