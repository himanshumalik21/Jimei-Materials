Our Manufacturing Excellence

Precision engineering and rigorous quality control at every step ensure superior ceramic substrate performance

1

Precision Machining & Via Formation

Advanced laser processing for DPC/DBC microvias (50-200μm) and green tape punching for HTCC via formation with ±10μm tolerance. Precision cutting for complex geometries across all ceramic substrate technologies.

2

Advanced Metallization Techniques

DC/RF sputtering for DPC thin-film deposition, high-temperature copper bonding for DBC (1065-1083°C), and screen printing of refractory metals (W/Mo) for HTCC conductor patterns—ensuring optimal adhesion and conductivity.

3

Circuit Patterning & Multilayer Processing

Fine-line photolithography for DPC circuits (20-50μm), precision etching for DBC power traces, and multilayer alignment/lamination for HTCC packages—delivering optimized layouts for RF, power, and 3D interconnect applications.

4

Surface Finishing & High-Temperature Processing

Electroplating for DPC/DBC surface finishes (ENIG/Immersion Ag/Sn) and controlled atmosphere co-firing for HTCC (1500-1600°C)—ensuring reliable performance, solderability, and hermeticity in demanding operating conditions.

5

Comprehensive Quality Control & Testing

AOI and X-ray inspection for DPC/DBC circuits, thermal cycling testing (-55°C to 150°C), and helium leak testing for HTCC hermeticity (<1×10-8 atm·cc/s). MIL-STD-883 compliance for aerospace and defense applications.

6

Final Inspection & Specialized Packaging

100% electrical testing and anti-static packaging for DPC/DBC substrates. Final visual inspection, Kovar lid sealing, and custom tray packaging for HTCC hermetic packages—ensuring damage-free delivery worldwide.

7

Thermal & Electrical Performance Optimization

RF performance optimization for DPC high-frequency circuits (up to 40GHz), thermal simulation for DBC power modules, and thermal stress analysis for HTCC extreme temperature cycling—ensuring optimal performance in all applications.

8

Custom Design & Engineering Support

Rapid prototyping and engineering support for DPC fine-feature circuits, DBC power module layouts, and HTCC multilayer designs—including material selection, thermal management solutions, and application-specific optimization.
